About Us

The vision for Charlie’s Garden is to create and maintain a beautiful, unique, safe, supportive and productive garden within Darlington for all residents. Charlie’s Garden promotes a sense of community where trust, effort, knowledge, skills and responsibility are shared; where creativity, quality and the environment are nurtured; and where equity and philanthropy can flourish.
