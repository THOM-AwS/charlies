Garden News & Events

Events

Working Bees – Currently on hold due to COVID Lockdown restrictions
Working Bee is held every Saturday morning from 9am to 10am, everyone is welcome to join.
Activities involves, plant new seedlings, watering the garden, weeding, feeding the worm farm, harvesting food/veg that is ready and maintaining the health of the garden.

Gardening Workshop

Our community garden is all about sharing knowledge, Judith our garden coordinator runs a Gardening Workshop for our community members once every 3 months. The workshop goes for 30min on topics such as; Soil health, how to maintain your worm farm, plant health, composting etc.

Stay turn to our next workshop by following us on Instagram & Facebook
